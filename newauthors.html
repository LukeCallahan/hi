Melinda Kohn

Around 20 years ago I won a handwriting analysis at one of those charity autions. When I met with the professional graphologist she agreed to look at five handwriting samples and give me a few minute description of each person. She told me things that were so accurate that it could not be just a coincidence. And she told me additional things that I did not know about these people, that I later found out to be true! When I asked the grraphologist what in the handwriting told here these things, she made it quite clear to me that telling me that was not part of the analyses and I would have to read books and take courses to learn this skill (you know, sort of like magicians will rarely tell you how a trick is done).

So off I went, reading scores of books and taking four coorespondence courses, learning all that I could about handwriting analysis. During this time at work I transferred to a new job and became an ombudsperson, a person who meets with people to help them resolve issues that they may have at work. I found that my handwriting skills were helpful in better understanding my clients.

Right about now I need to let you know that I do not have that great a memory, so that remembering what all these writing traits meant was getting to be quite a bother. I worked to develop a simple tool for myself using the handwriting traits that I found were most useful. I found that the 11 traits that are in the free course form a solid foundation for a basic understanding of a person's personality. I worded the interpretation of these traits in a way that was descriptive and not judgmental, so that people would be more willing to accept and make use of what they may learn.

I have helped so many people better understand themselves and their others by looking at their handwriting, that I wanted to make this simple skill more broadly available. So I developed and was granted a patent on Handwriting Insights, and I am marketing it here. I hope that you find it useful!

 

 

Paula Sassi, C.G.

The basic premise of this website is that having a basic knowledge of handwriting analysis is a very useful skill to have. It helps us to better understand others and ourself, it is a phenominal conversations starter, and it lots of fun at parties.

But DON'T confuse what you have learned here with a professional skill level in handwriting analysis. That takes years of study and lots of experience.
