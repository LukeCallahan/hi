Handwriting Insights is easy to use. Handwriting has different characteristics (such as size) for which there are different variations (large, small, really small). Handwriting Insights depicts each characteristic through illustrations and descriptions. Simply match the handwriting sample being analyzed to one of the samples for each characteristic, and Handwriting Insights does the rest.

Below is a basic demonstration of the concept behind Handwriting Insights. Pick a sample of handwriting, either your own or someone else's, and for each of the three characteristics below, click the button below the variation that best matches the sample. When you're finished, click on the "Create Analysis" button to see the results of your personalized demonstration.
